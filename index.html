Street Harassment in Wales

A Dissertation project by Vanesa Terzieva

Ask a woman if she had ever experienced street harassment in her life and you will be told stories of the times she has been bombarded with sexual remarks on the way home from the bus stop. You will be told of the times she was cat-called by strangers, and the times she wished she hadn’t gone out of the house that day. You will hear stories of being groped on public transport, of having to cross the road because of a construction site, of having to keep keys in hand and headphones in the pocket. You will hear of how she is afraid to wear certain clothes now, how she is scared to walk alone, or take the bus alone, or go outside of the house alone. And you will hear how during a brief encounter that might have lasted less than a full minute, she had counted all the exits, calculated all the routes and had one persistent thought go through her mind countless times- the thought that she might not make it home today…
